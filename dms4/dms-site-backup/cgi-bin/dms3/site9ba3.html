DMS3 FORUMS

The DMS3 'Dev.olution Memory System' is the most advanced upgrade solution to your PS2 recordable media playback requirements needs. Our device has been designed to exacting standards.


Rest assured that you will enjoy bullet-proof reliability and stability from our design. We have set the standard for design by operating within the safe power requirement limitations, avoiding placing stress on the internal architecture of the PS2!

A rigorous testing procedure and expert development team has led to the completion of this very fine design. Customers will enjoy a future proof design since our device is flash upgradable, by downloading the latest CD update from our web.


Furthermore we have taken our research and development outside the normal parameters by integrating the DMS feature, see "Specs" page for further info.

Two other world firsts in Direct boot MOD chip design have also been achieved! We have made possible the playback of any region DVD movies on the PAL PS2's and have also unlocked USA/ASIAN consoles to playback other territory NTSC movies!
As a bonus we've added support of standard IDE HDD's when used in conjunction with the BBA. Now you can dump files and utils, format and manage your HDD with the DMS HDD Explorer and associated tools (Grab them from our downloads section). The latest flash (1.8) also has a convenient "quick boot" mode, enabled by holding Select which skips the boot logo and goes straight to the game.

We also engineered the device to remove green screen. The DMS3 is well placed to cater for everyone's needs. It is fully compatible with every* PS2 model available in the market including the notoriously awkward JAP 10000 and 15000 models. There is no need to configure our device to your version of console. An inbuilt detection function is included to ensure hassle free installation.


Thanks to our development team and everyone who supported us in DMS3 project!

* Please note that the v9/v10 revisions are currently supported only by DMS3 V9.
