12.12.2005

We have uploaded the official DMS flash version 0.6 which can be found in the downloads section. Changes since the last official flash release include support for DMS4 SE series chips and the V14 consoles.

Due to variations in the numerous V14 revisions, some which were unavailable to us before completion of our development, users installing DMS4 S.E. Lite and Pro in a V14 console will be required to download this latest official DMS firmware and reflash their mod using the fastboot method. Fastboot can be enabled by holding SELECT on the joypad during the boot process. This is necessary due to an issue with the factory firmware which prevents homebrew discs from booting on Japanese and European V14 consoles unless fastboot is used (V1-V13 consoles are NOT affected). Once you have updated the firmware on the flash, it will no longer be necessary to carry out this procedure. The console will then be able to boot your homebrew applications without any interaction on your part whatsoever.

Needless to say that we have already revised the firmware, and the next batch of DMS4 S.E. Lite and Pro's to be shipped will not be affected by this issue.
